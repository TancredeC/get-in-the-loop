Distorted Sensory Perception (DSP) is the vinyl imprint and the brain child
of the electronic outfit Spacial Zone. The duo consists of Zobol & Entrave,
joined by Oshkossh, half of the Transitional State and Mindless Evolving Objects
outfit, who decided to venture out into the world of independent record labels to
further release their own music, on their terms. Their aim is to showcase honest
forward thinking electronic music from talented friends and affiliated artists.
DSP hails its roots in weird, wonderful analogue techno, electro and progressive
form of electronic music. DSP embraces new and intuitive music that has something real to say.
